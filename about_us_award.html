Awards & Recognitions

  • 2016

    The Lee Kum Kee Family donated USD 21 million to establish the Lee Kum Sheung Center for Health and Happiness at Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health.

    LKKHPG was named one of the “Best Companies to Work for in Asia 2016”.

    LKKHPG claimed “Outstanding Corporate Social Responsibility Award” byThe Mirror for the fifth time.

  • 2015

    Infinitus (China) Company Ltd. was certified by the Top Employers Institute as a Top Employer China 2016.

    Infinitus (China) Company Ltd. and the University of Cambridge jointly established the Cambridge Infinitus Research Center.

    LKKHPG claimed “Outstanding Corporate Social Responsibility Award” byThe Mirror for the fourth time.

    LKKHPG expanded its business into new territories and established Naturale (China) Pharma Co., Ltd. and HeHa Digital Health Ltd., marking its entry into the fields of Chinese herbal plantation and mobile digitalization.

    Infinitus (China) was granted the internationally renowned quality awards by Monde Selection, one of the most historic and authoritative third-party testing institutes in the world

    Recognized as “National High-Tech Enterprise” for the third time along with its first accreditation in 2008 and the re-examination in 2011

  • 2014

    According to the evaluation of World Brand Lab, the brand value of Infinitus stood at RMB 36.889 billion, ranking 46th among China’s 500 Most Valuable Brands.

    Infinitus (China) Company Ltd. was granted with the honorary title of "Top 100 Creditable Demonstration Units of China Food Safety".

    The Infinitus Yingkou Production Base was officially completed and put into operation.

    Our Chairman, Mr. Sammy Lee was honored the "2014 Anti-drug Philanthropist of China" by China National Narcotics Commission (NNCC) and China Narcotics Control Foundation (CNCF)

    Infinitus (China) was crowned “Key Large-Sized Enterprise” in Guangdong, China.

  • 2013

    “Chinese Best Corporate Citizen” by 21st Century Business Herald and 21st Century Business Review

    “Golden Bee Excellent CSR Report Award Evergreen Enterprise”

    “Top 100 Creditable Demonstration Units of China Food Safety” for the 11th time

    “Best Corporate Brand Image Award”

    “Top 100 Business Model of China Food Safety”
