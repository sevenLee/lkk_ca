Infinitus Product Category

The systematic modernization of Tradition Chinese Medicine in recent decades, and its integration with Western medicine, has given rise to a new healthcare model increasingly adopted around the world. We are dedicated to develop an entire line of revolutionary products with emphasis on health promotion and disease prevention based on findings from the latest scientific research on the integration of modern Western medicine and Traditional Chinese medicine. With annual investment of RMB 100 million, the various joint collaboration initiatives have yielded six major brands with more than 80 products in the line-up. We hold the right to 35 patents on core technologies that involved complex polysaccharides, Schisandrin B, compound whitening factor, active peptides, plant glycoside, epidermal growth factor and sapidus saponins, etc.

  • Health & Food
Infinitus health products are developed based on TCM concepts. High-tech extracted natural plant essences are combined with traditional Chinese herbs to stimulate the body’s immunity and resistance. By adjusting the physiological functions of all body parts, organs will recover through self-regulation and self-repairing. Infinitus health products have been well received by consumers because of their high quality, safety and efficiency.
